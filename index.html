Gameplay Update
1.00
General Updates
  • Reverted heroes starting with higher base stats and having better stat growth. Back to vanilla values.
  • Strength now increases health per point by 30, instead of 20. Health regeneration from strength increased from 0.1 to 0.15.
  • Heroes now start with +800 base health and +300 base mana.
  • Attribute scaling levels from 7 to 10. Improved attribute scaling from +12% to +80%
  • Changed how Agility increases attack speed. The first 100 agility gives 100 attack speed (vanilla scaling), but every point afterwards has diminishing returns. Attack speed doubles every time your agility is increased ten fold. (100 Agility = 100 attack speed and 1000 Agility = 200 Attack Speed)
  • Changed how Agility increases armor. The first 100 agility gives 16.7 armor (vanilla scaling), but every point afterwards has diminishing returns. Armor doubles every time your agility is increased ten fold. (100 Agility = 16.7 armor and 1000 Agility = 33.4 Armor )
Item Updates
  • Health booster items now stack with each other.
  • Reduced armor on various items.
Dagon
  • Added a Dagon Line. Combines with Hourglass Fragment at Tier 2. Upgrades using the Mystic Ring line.
Guardian Greaves
  • Reworked. Now combines with Holy Locket. Guardian Greaves upgrades no longer heal a percentage of maximum health, instead they gain stacks every interval or when spells are cast by enemies. The healing and mana restoration is increased for every stack. Provides outgoing healing amp.
Hourglass Fragment
  • Added to the game. Costs 420 gold.
  • Provides 10% cooldown reduction.

Signet of Destiny
  • Added to the game. Recipe is Holy Locket, Kaya, Dagon 5 and Spirit Vessel.
  • Lets you cast a burst of magic on enemies an allies, healing allies and damaging enemies. The unit is afterwards either buffed or debuffed.
  • Enemies affected by the debuff take magical damage and lose a percentage of their current health every second. Also have all healing reduced.
  • Allies affected by the buff restore health equal to the magic damage an enemy would take and regain a percentage of their missing health. Also have all healing increased.
  • Initial healing and damage is increased by the amount of charges.
  • You get charges every interval, when enemies cast spells and when any unit dies. Units dying gives the most charges.
Yasha and Kaya
  • Added to the game.
  • Tier 2 Combines with Hourglass Fragment; granting some base cooldown reduction. Has following passive as well: Spellsword - Every time you attack you get stacks of Archon. Every stack of Archon reduces the cast time and cooldown of the next spell cast up to a maximum, consuming all stacks of Archon. Casting a spell gives you an attack speed buff.
  • Yasha and Kaya 3 combines using Mask of Madness in the recipe. The Madness effect no longer silences you, and its lifesteal restores an amount of mana equal to 5% of the health gained.
  • Yasha and Kaya 4 combines using Void's Reach into the recipe.
  • Yasha and Kaya 5 combines using Planar Anvil into the recipe. Loses Static Charge, and gains Wild Static instead. While Wild Magic is active, the initial Chain Lightning hits multiple targets.
