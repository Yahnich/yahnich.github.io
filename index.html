Gameplay Update
1.00
General Updates
  • Reverted heroes starting with higher base stats and having better stat growth. Back to vanilla values.
  • Strength now increases health per point by 33, instead of 22. Health regeneration from strength increased from 0.1 to 0.15.
  • Heroes now start with +800 base health and +300 base mana.
  • Attribute scaling levels from 7 to 10. Improved attribute scaling from +12% to +80%
  • Changed how Agility increases attack speed. The first 100 agility gives 100 attack speed (vanilla scaling), but every point afterwards has diminishing returns. Attack speed doubles every time your agility is increased ten fold. (100 Agility = 100 attack speed and 1000 Agility = 200 Attack Speed)
  • Changed how Agility increases armor. The first 100 agility gives 16.7 armor (vanilla scaling), but every point afterwards has diminishing returns. Armor doubles every time your agility is increased ten fold. (100 Agility = 16.7 armor and 1000 Agility = 33.4 Armor )
  • Changed how Intelligence increases magic resistance. The first 100 intelligence gives 10% (vanilla scaling), but every point afterwards has diminishing returns. Magic resistance doubles every time your intelligence is increased ten fold. (100 Intelligence = 10% Magic Resistance and 1000 Intelligence = 20% Magic Resistance )
  • MMR loss and gain is no longer based on personal MMR, instead it's based on group MMR. When a player abandons, this adjusts the MMR gain/loss for everyone as well.
  • Abandons are now a fixed -120 MMR (maximum penalty).
  • When bosses are ignored, they get pretty upset. 90 seconds after spawning, boss damage scales up every 30 seconds, if they have not taken at least 2.4% of their maximum health in damage over that period. Every 30 seconds where this is true, their movement speed, attack speed and all damage they deal increases by 30%. You can remove stacks by either dealing 4.8% of their maximum health, removing 1 stack for every 4.8% maximum health they lost, or when a hero dies. A hero dying resets the Enrage timer to 90 seconds.
  • Neutrals spawn when a round starts.
  • Neutral gold no longer scales with time, instead scales with round.
  • After clearing a neutral camp, the next neutral camp will spawn with Ancestor's Vengeance. Ancestor's Vengeance reduces the damage taken by the creeps and increases all damage they deal. Upon entering a new round, Ancestor's Vengeance is cleared from existing creeps and the strength is reset to 0. Only works for the neutral team, upon taking over a creep buffed with Ancestor's Vengeance, the buff is lost.
  • Neutrals level up their abilities after round 6, 12, 17 and 21.
  • Neutrals no longer drop neutral items.
  • Everyone starts with a Tier 1 Neutral Token
  • You get a Tier 2 Neutral Token round 6.
  • You get a Tier 3 Neutral Token round 12.
  • You get a Tier 4 Neutral Token round 17.
  • You get a Tier 5 Neutral Token round 21.
