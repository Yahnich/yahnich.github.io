Gameplay Update
1.00a
General Updates
  • Neutral health and damage slightly reduced, reduced neutral ability strength.
  • Creep-type unit spawns no longer provide gold or experience when killed.
Item Updates
  • Replaced all sources of cooldown reduction in items by Cast Speed. Cast Speed reduces seconds per cast by reducing cooldown and increasing casting speed. Formula is the same as attack speed. Overall this means that 1 item with cooldown reduction will provide a similar amount of value compared to previous patch, but stacking them has diminishing returns.
  • Yasha and Kaya no longer provides cast time and cooldown reduction, now provides cast speed.
  • To account for doubling physical damage, lifesteal has been halved on items. Creep lifesteal reduction from 40% to 10%
