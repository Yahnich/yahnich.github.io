Gameplay Update
1.00
General Updates
  • In Nightmare Difficulty, you no longer get to pick from all heroes. Instead, 20 heroes are randomly made available. This change allows you to pick duplicate heroes.
  • Rescaled level gain from rounds. Overall level gain is less spikey (ex. Round 12). More levels earlygame.
  • Rescaled attack speed from Agility: Rescaled vanilla scaling from 1 attack speed per Agility to 0.75 attack speed per agility. After 100 Agility you need 5x (down from 10x) Agility to double attack speed. Previous values were 100/200/400 attack speed for 100/1000/10000 total Agility, new values are 75/150/300 attack speed for 100/500/2500 Agility OR alternatively 75/200/545 attack speed for 100/1000/10000 total Agility
  • Rescaled armor from Agility: Reduced vanilla scaling from 1.66 armor per 10 Agility to 0.65 armor per 10 Agility (6.5 at 100 Agility), after 100 Agility you need 5x (down from 10x) Agility to double armor. Previous values were 16.6/33.3/66.6 armor for 100/1000/10000 total Agility, new values are 6.5/13/26 armor for 100/500/2500 Agility OR alternatively 6.5/17.6/47.5 armor for 100/1000/10000 total Agility
  • Rescaled magic resistance from intelligence: Reduced vanilla scaling from 1% per 10 Intelligence to 0.4% per 10 Intelligence (4% at 100 Intelligence), after 100 Intelligence you need 5x (down from 10x) Intelligence to double the magic resistance. Previous values were 10%/20%/40% magic resistance for 100/1000/10000 total Intelligence, new values are 4%/8%/16% magic resistance for 100/500/2500 Intelligence OR alternatively 6%/10.8%/29% for 100/1000/10000 total Intelligence.
  • Intelligence no longer provides Spell Amplification. Instead, the hero's Primary Attribute gives 1.5% spell amplification per 100 Primary Attributes. Universal heroes recieve 0.7% Spell Amp per 100 of Any Attribute (2.1% from +100 all stats).
  • Reduced bonus base damage per level from 2 x HeroLevel^2 to 1.5 x HeroLevel^2 (25% reduction at all levels)
  • Neutral creeps now have 7 levels for their skills and their skills are leveled every 3 rounds (lv2 at round 4, lv3 at round 7 and so on until lv7 is reached at round 19)
Item Updates
  • All changes applied to hero abilities have also been applied to items.
  • Nerfed some T1 Neutrals strength to be more in line with T1 Shop Items, buffed others.
  • Nerfed all T2 Neutrals strength to be more in line with T2 Shop Items, mostly to their stats, some passive effects were buffed.
  • Nerfed some T3 Neutrals strength to be more in line with T3 Shop Items, buffed others.
  • Gungnir items no longer are amped by spell amp in line with Monkey King Bar.
  • Removed attack speed upgraders, items built out of attack speed upgraders reduced in price slightly.
  • Removed crit upgraders and their recipes. Total price of Desolator and Silver Edge unchanged.
  • Rescaled Desolator critical damage scaling to be more linear (prior to patch, certain tier ups had a disproportionate damage jump). Desolator receives Critical Damage scaling starting from Desolator 2, instead of 3
  • Desolator debuff doesn't stack with different tiers, highest armor reduction takes priority.
  • Rescaled Silver Edge critical damage scaling to be more linear (prior to patch, certain tier ups had a disproportionate damage jump).
  • Battle Fury Cleave standardized to function like DOTA's Cleave (Cone instead of circle).
  • Fused Spear of Justice and Battle Fury lines into one.
  • Added following active to Butterfly: Untouchable. While active, Butterfly's evasion is doubled.
  • Windhose (Butterfly 2) now includes Yasha in the recipe. Windhose passively provides movement speed and when attacking the same target, you gain stacks of Zephyr, which increase your movement and attack speed.
  • Blooddrinker (Butterfly 3) adds Morbid Mask to the recipe and gives its owner Lifesteal. Zephyr's passive improved the Crimson Zephyr, which increases Blooddrinker's lifesteal for each stack.
  • Divine Butterfly Untouchable now includes the bonuses from prior upgrades, magic resistance gain is now based on the evasion after multiplier.
  • Bahamut's Fang now does the following: Upon reaching maximum stacks of Zephyr, you gain +50% Agility.
  • Asura Rapier has all of the effects, just more stats.
Hero Updates
  • Increased base damage for all heroes by another 30 (from +30 to +60) and base health by another 500 (from 1500 to 2000).
  • Reduced attribute growth from 50% to 25% to account for all the bonuses heroes get as a baseline now.
  • Drastically adjusted spell damage numbers on all abilities for all heroes to align them with the new increased spell amplification numbers. Damage floors of abilities has been increased, damage ceiling reduced.
  • Damage of area of effect spells has been scaled down to 75%/60% damage for spells that are likely to hit 2/3 hero units.
  • Cooldowns of damage abilities have all been normalized to their 4th level for basic abilities and 3rd level for ultimate abilities. This significantly lowers cooldowns for all damaging abilities on early levels (no more abilities with 42s cooldown at lv1 which scale up to normal cooldowns).
  • All abilities now only benefit from one source of scaling. Abilities that scale off of maximum health (Ex. Fire Storm), attack damage (Ex. Multishot), damage dealt (Ex. Disseminate) and so on do not benefit from spell amplification. If an ability has a base damage component and a scaling damage componenent, the base damage still benefits from spell amplification (Ex. Doom's Infernal Blade).
  • All abilities that scaled off of primary attribute (like Centaur's Double Edge) no longer have bonus damage based off of that attribute. Abilities that scaled off of primary attribute had their base damage adjusted so the damage output is similar before and after the patch.
  • Doom: Devour no longer applies a damage multiplier to creep skills, instead creep skills are set to the level of Devour.

  • Round 17: Wraith King boss only summons Tiny Skeletons.
